Cacio E Pepe

My family loved this pasta! It was delicious and not too difficult to make. I used the recipe from Bon Appétit. The ingrediants are:

Kosher salt

6 oz. pasta (such as egg tagliolini, bucatini, or spaghetti) 3 Tbsp. unsalted butter, cubed, divided 1 tsp. freshly cracked black pepper ¾ cup finely grated Grana Padano or Parmesan ⅓ cup finely grated Pecorino
